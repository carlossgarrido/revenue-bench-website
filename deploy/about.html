The thesis

Most recruiters find resumes. We find revenue.

A resume tells you where someone has been. It does not tell you whether they can perform in your role, against your buyer, inside your sales motion. Most recruiters cannot tell the difference, because they have never built or run a sales team.

We have. Revenue Bench was founded by people who have installed sales systems and built and exited revenue teams. We assess talent the way an operator would, so we recognize what predicts performance long before it shows up in the numbers.

What we believe

The principles behind every search.

01

Assess before you hire

Interviewing well is not the same as performing. We measure will to sell, sales DNA, and process fit with Objective Management Group, around 95% predictive validity, before anyone makes an offer. Hiring on instinct alone picks a top performer only about one time in five.

02

Onboarding is where hires are won or lost

The first 90 days decide whether a hire performs or stalls. So a dedicated coach works with the hire and the manager weekly, every time. We do not hand off and hope.

03

Specialists, not generalists

We do one thing: revenue talent. Sellers, leaders, enablement, and RevOps. We do not staff every function, because depth in the role is what makes the match right.

04

We win when the hire works

Our 90-day replacement guarantee puts our name on the outcome. If a hire leaves or is not performing within 90 days, we run the search again at no additional fee.
