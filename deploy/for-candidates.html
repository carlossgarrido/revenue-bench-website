Questions candidates ask

What it is like to be on the bench.

Is there a cost to candidates?

No. There is never a cost to candidates. We are paid by the companies that hire from the bench. Your assessment, your matching, and your 90-day coaching are all included at no cost to you.

What is the assessment?

Every candidate completes an evaluation with Objective Management Group, the most validated sales-specific assessment available. It measures will to sell, sales DNA, and how you execute, so we can match you to a role where your strengths actually win, rather than guessing from a resume.

What is the 90-day coaching?

After you are placed, a dedicated coach works with you weekly for your first 90 days. The cadence covers role clarity, method, pipeline build, and deal execution, so you ramp faster and are fully performing in the role by day 90.

What kinds of teams will I be matched to?

Teams that take revenue seriously: companies with a defined sales process, real support, and operators who know what good looks like. We match you to the motion and environment where your strengths pay off, not just the next open req.
